FAQ

How do I transition to a new food?

Go slow over 7–10 days: start ~25% new / 75% old, then increase by 25% every few days. If you see loose stool, slow down.

What if my dog has allergies?

Try limited‑ingredient or single‑protein recipes. Keep a food diary and consult your vet for long‑term care.

Are there cheaper ways to upgrade?

Use fresh toppers over quality kibble, rotate proteins, and buy larger plans to reduce cost per meal.
