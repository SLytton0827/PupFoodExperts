FAQ

How do I transition to a new food?

Go slow over 7–10 days. Start ~25% new / 75% old, then increase by 25% every few days. Watch for loose stool or vomiting and slow down if needed.

What if my dog has allergies?

Look for limited‑ingredient or single‑protein recipes. Keep a food diary and consult your vet for elimination trials and long‑term care.

Are there cheaper ways to upgrade?

Try 50/50 fresh toppers over quality kibble, rotate proteins, and buy larger plans to reduce cost per meal.
